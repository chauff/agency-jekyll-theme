Sponsoring and Contact

DIR is an annual event which is attended by approx. 100 researchers and practitioners, among them many of the top PhD students in the IR field from the Netherlands and Belgium. It offers a number of sponsorship options, which allow organizations to gain visibility in the community and contribute to the success of the workshop. DIR is an ideal place to advertise an organisation's involvement in the field of IR and reach students for potential employment opportunities.

If your organisation is interested in sponsoring DIR, please contact dr. Claudia Hauff at c.hauff[at]tudelft.nl.

The workshop is organized by members of TU Delft's Web Information System's group: Claudia Hauff, Dan Davis and Guanliang Chen.
